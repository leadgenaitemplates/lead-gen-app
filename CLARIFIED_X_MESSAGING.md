## What NOT to Say

❌ "Get 50 B2B leads in CSV"  
(Sounds like you're replacing Apollo. You're not.)

❌ "No setup required"  
(They still need their Apollo API keys to enrich. Be honest.)

❌ "50 verified B2B leads"  
(They're company-verified, not contact-verified. Apollo does that.)

❌ "The fastest lead gen tool"  
(You're not a lead gen tool. You're a search tool FOR lead gen.)
